Lasagna recipe

Quick and easy Italian lasagna

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish known for its layers of tender pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese.

Each bite offers a delightful combination of flavors and textures, from the savory, aromatic sauce to the gooey, golden cheese topping. This hearty and comforting meal is perfect for gatherings, providing a satisfying and delicious experience that leaves a lasting impression.

Ingredients:

    Meat sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

    • Cheese mixture:

  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

    • For the layers:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    • Optional:

  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps:

    Step 1: Prepare the Meat Sauce

  1. Brown the Ground Beef: In a large skillet or pot, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add Aromatics: Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the beef. Cook for about 5 minutes, until the onion is softened and translucent.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer uncovered for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in chopped fresh parsley towards the end. Remove from heat and set aside.

    4. Step 2: Prepare the Cheese Mixture

  4. Mix Ricotta Cheese: In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and chopped parsley. Mix until smooth and well combined. Set aside.

    5. Step 3: Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  5. Boil Noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente (firm but tender). Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.

    6. Step 4: Assemble the Lasagna

  6. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Layering: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping them.
  8. Layer Cheese and Sauce: Spread a third of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with a third of the meat sauce and sprinkle with a third of the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. Repeat Layers: Repeat the layers twice more, starting with noodles, then ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and cheeses.

    10. Step 5: Bake the Lasagna

  10. Cover and Bake: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, tenting it slightly to prevent the cheese from sticking. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
  11. Uncover and Bake: Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.

    12. Step 6: Rest and Serve

  12. Rest: Let the lasagna rest for about 15 minutes before slicing and serving. This allows the layers to set and makes it easier to cut into neat portions.
  13. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
*This lasagna recipe serves about 8-10 people, depending on portion size. It's perfect for a hearty family dinner or special occasion meal.

Pesto Primavera Penne recipe

Quick and easy pesto primavera penne

Pesto Primavera Penne is a vibrant Italian pasta dish that combines al dente penne with a medley of fresh, seasonal vegetables.

Tossed in a fragrant basil pesto sauce, this dish offers a delightful harmony of flavors and textures, perfect for a light yet satisfying meal. Garnished with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and a touch of lemon zest, Pesto Primavera Penne is a celebration of simple, wholesome ingredients.

Ingredients:

    For the Pasta and Vegetables:

  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup asparagus, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced (use a mix of red, yellow, and green)
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the Pesto Sauce:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of half a lemon (optional, for extra freshness)

    • For Garnish:

  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Lemon zest
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps:

    Step 1: Cook the Pasta

  1. Boil Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

    2. Step 2: Prepare the Vegetables

  2. Sauté Vegetables: In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the asparagus, zucchini, bell peppers, and broccoli. Sauté for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  3. Add Tomatoes and Spinach: minutes until the spinach is wilted and the tomatoes are slightly softened. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and set aside.

    4. Step 3: Make the Pesto Sauce

  4. Blend Ingredients: In a food processor or blender, combine the fresh basil leaves, grated Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic. Pulse until finely chopped.
  5. Add Olive Oil: With the processor running, slowly pour in the extra-virgin olive oil until the mixture is smooth and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add lemon juice if desired for extra freshness.

    6. Step 4: Combine Pasta, Vegetables, and Pesto

  6. Mix Together: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked penne pasta, sautéed vegetables, and pesto sauce. Toss until the pasta and vegetables are evenly coated with the pesto.

    7. Step 5: Serve

  7. Garnish: Transfer the Pesto Primavera Penne to a serving platter or individual plates. Garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese, lemon zest, and fresh basil leaves.
  8. Enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy this vibrant and flavorful dish!

*This recipe serves about 4-6 people and is perfect for a light, fresh, and delicious meal.

Caprese Salad recipe

Fresh caprese salad

Caprese Salad is a simple and refreshing Italian dish that highlights the flavors of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves.

Drizzled with extra-virgin olive oil and a splash of balsamic vinegar, this dish is perfect as a light appetizer or side salad. The combination of ingredients creates a harmonious blend of textures and tastes that epitomize the essence of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

    Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

  1. Slice the ripe tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into even rounds.

    2. Step 2: Layer

  2. On a large serving platter, arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices alternately, overlapping slightly. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.

    3. Step 3: Season

  3. Drizzle the extra-virgin olive oil over the arranged salad. If desired, drizzle a small amount of balsamic vinegar for extra flavor. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

    4. Step 4: Serve

  4. Serve immediately or let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Enjoy as a light appetizer or a side dish.
*This Caprese Salad serves 4 people, providing a quick and delightful option for those seeking a fresh and easy Italian dish.
